Structured training for

TrainerKit connects to your indoor cycling trainer via Bluetooth for FTP-based structured workouts. Multi-athlete support, workout library, Workout builder, and seamless exports of completed workouts.

Available for macOS
Features

Built for serious training

Smart Trainer Control

Bluetooth connectivity to control resistance on your smart trainer in real-time during structured workouts.

Multi-Athlete Support

Individual profiles with separate FTP values, workout histories, and platform connections for each athlete.

Workout Library

Stock templates for endurance, sweet spot, threshold, VO2max, and more. Create custom workouts with the built-in editor.

FTP-Based Zones

Workouts defined as percentage of FTP. Power targets adjust automatically when you switch athletes or update your FTP.

FTP Testing

Built-in ramp test protocol to measure your functional threshold power with guided execution and result tracking.

Workout History

Complete history of completed workouts with metrics including TSS, normalized power, and performance data.
